Anil Kumar: Leader in Cruise Ship Careers and Global Hospitality

Anil Kumar, Managing Director, is a visionary in cruise ship careers and global hospitality. From 2004 to 2014, he built a strong foundation with Royal Caribbean Cruise Company. Drawing on that decade of experience, he established a successful venture connecting talent with careers at sea. With over 11 years in cruise ship recruitment, Anil has helped place 500+ professionals on top cruise lines. His passion for empowering individuals and setting high standards continues to shape the future of cruise industry employment.
