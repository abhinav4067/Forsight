Course Duration:

Total Duration: 1 Year

Format: 2 Semesters (6 months each)

Includes: Theory classes, practical training, industry visits, and internship

Core Subjects Covered:

1. Front Office Operations
Guest handling, check-in/check-out procedures, reservations
Use of property management systems (PMS)

2. Food & Beverage Service
Restaurant service techniques
Table setup, menu planning, and guest service etiquette

3. Food Production (Kitchen Operations)
Basics of culinary arts
Indian and international cuisines
Kitchen safety, hygiene, and equipment handling

4. Housekeeping Operations
Room maintenance, laundry management
Cleaning methods and materials, inventory control

5. Communication & Soft Skills
English for hospitality
Customer service and team dynamics

6. Hospitality Sales & Marketing Basics (Optional/Elective)
Introduction to sales strategies in the hotel industry

Hotel Management

The proposed 1-Year Diploma in Hotel Management is a job-oriented program designed to provide students with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills across key departments of the hospitality industry. The course is structured to balance classroom instruction with hands-on training, preparing students for immediate employment or further specialization.
